Shipping & Delivery Policy

For domestic buyers, orders are shipped through registered domestic courier companies and
/or speed post only. Orders are shipped within 6-7 Days days or as per the delivery date
agreed at the time of order confirmation and delivering of the shipment subject to Courier
Company / post office norms.

HARMONY CHOCOLATES/ AAYUSH SAPRA is not liable for any delay in delivery by the
courier company / postal authorities and only guarantees to hand over the consignment to the
courier company or postal authorities within 6-7 Days days from the date of the order and
payment or as per the delivery date agreed at the time of order confirmation.

Delivery of all orders will be to the address provided by the buyer. Delivery of our services will
be confirmed on your mail ID as specified during registration.

The onus is on the customer to provide accurate delivery information at the time of ordering.
Delivery delays due to incorrect/incomplete addresses will not be the responsibility of "Ooty
Chocolates".

Force Majeure and Unforeseen Circumstances- Timelines mentioned in the policy document
will be undertaken in a best effort basis. However, if there are delays due to unforeseen
circumstances, including but not limited to strikes, bandhs, festival holidays, natural
calamities, Harmony chocolates will not be held responsible.

feedback@harmonychocolates.in.
